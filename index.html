Casa Lari is an exceptional interior design studio located in the beautiful city of Barcelona. Founded by Alice, an experienced and talented interior designer, the studio is renowned for its elegant and contemporary designs that perfectly capture the essence of Mediterranean living.
Casa Lari's design philosophy emphasizes the use of natural materials and neutral colors to create harmonious and comfortable spaces that reflect the personality and lifestyle of their clients. Whether it's a modern apartment, a traditional villa, or a commercial space, Casa Lari's team of experts work closely with their clients to create bespoke designs that exceed their expectations. With their impeccable taste, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence, Casa Lari is one of the most sought-after interior design studios in Barcelona.
